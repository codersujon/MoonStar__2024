Our Company

Company at a glance

Moon Star Publicity was established in 1987 with only 6 staffs. Since then our business has expanded gradually. Now we have almost 26 staffs at the management. We have all the modern technologies & equipment in the factory for the betterment of our clients and compete in the competitive market with pride. We have almost 300 Billboard sites throughout the country.

We can assure you that every customer is valuable client to us and can rest the assurance about the quality of outdoor advertising and printing facilities.

Mission

Keeping pace with the new developments and changes in media and advertising methods as well as holding our standards, professionalism, and the quality of work; our mission is to keep our company’s pride upheld in the competitive market.
